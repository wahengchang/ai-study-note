# 行銷 Prompt 範本

> 回到 [Prompt 範本集索引](./index.md) | [研究筆記](../google-workspace-gemini-prompt-guide.md)

## 品牌視覺開發

```
Generate ideas for a creative and eye-catching logo for my new business,
a coffee shop combined with a video game cafe. Generate a logo considering
the following:
Dual Concept: The logo needs to clearly signal both the coffee and gaming
aspects of the business without being too cluttered.
Target Audience: Appeal to a wide range of gamers (casual and enthusiast),
as well as coffee lovers seeking a unique hangout spot.
Style Options: I'm open to these approaches — let's get a few examples in
each of these three styles to compare: Modern and Playful, Retro-Cool,
Sleek and Minimalist.
```
**工具**：Gemini App

### 迭代：命名與標語

```
Write a tagline and 10 potential names for the business to go with
these logos.
```

## 品牌策略

```
I am a brand manager at [company]. Help me define a clear and effective
brand architecture for [company], considering its diverse products and
service portfolio.
For additional context, here is our current brand portfolio: [List all
existing brands, products, and services]. Here is our company mission and
vision: [overview]. And these are our target audience(s): [describe].
Our desired brand positioning is [explain].
```

## 市場研究

```
I need to do market research on [industry] industry to identify new trends.
Use [URLs] to uncover emerging trends and shifting consumer preferences.
```

### A/B 測試文案

```
I need to A/B test new messaging. Here is our messaging: [messaging].
Generate three different variations of ad copy.
```

## SEM 關鍵字與廣告文案

```
I am a digital marketing manager at [company]. I am working on SEM ads
for [product]. Here are my seed keywords: [list keywords]. Help me generate
a list of additional keywords and long-tail keywords and phrases that can
help me maximize ad performance.
```

### 廣告文案變體

```
For my SEM campaign, use these keywords as inspiration to generate multiple
ad copy variations with different headlines, descriptions, and calls to
action for [product]. Use a [tone] tone in the copy.
```

### 針對不同受眾調整

```
Do the same thing, except write new options for [audience], adjust the tone
to be [tone] and focus the copy on highlighting [feature] of [product].
```

> **進階：Gem "SEM Ad Copy Generator"** — 建立廣告結構、語調、KPI 指令，快速產出變體。

## 內容行銷

### 部落格起草

```
Create a blog draft announcing that [name] is joining [company] as
[position]. [Share two or three details from their bio].
```

### 五步驟 Nurture Email

```
Create a 5-step nurture email cadence to [prospective customers] who have
signed up for [our newsletter], with the goal of getting them to [purchase]
[product] using @[Product Specific Notes] and @[Product FAQ].
```

### Landing Page 文案

```
Create compelling copy for a landing page promoting a new
[ebook/webinar/free trial and details] designed for an executive target
audience. Highlight key benefits and encourage conversions with persuasive
calls to action.
```

## 社群媒體

```
Write three engaging social media posts about [product/service/topic] that
would appeal to [target audience]. Keep each social media post to two
sentences and include a call to action to visit [our website].
```

```
Create a social media post promoting our upcoming [event name]. Include
attention-grabbing language and relevant hashtags for [audience].
```

> **進階：Gem "Social Media Copywriter"** — 指定品牌語調、hashtag 策略、目標受眾。

## 受眾研究與 Persona

```
I am a marketing specialist focused on [area] at [company]. I need to
conduct in-depth audience research so that I can develop convincing
marketing artifacts for [personas]. To start, help me generate a
comprehensive profile of [target audience]. Include core demographics and
psychographics, online platforms they frequent, key pain points [product]
could solve, and language and messaging that resonates with them.
```

## 行銷計劃

```
I'm developing a marketing plan for a new app that provides [functionality].
My target audience is [audience]. Help me create a plan with a focus on
[marketing channels]. Here's what I'd like you to cover: competitor
analysis, ideal marketing channel mix with rationale, budget
recommendations, key messaging ideas, and proposed campaign timeline
with KPIs.
```

## 視覺素材

```
Help me create inspirational images for a marketing campaign for [type of
product]. Images should use [colors] and [natural elements, such as
clouds]. Use a [photorealistic] style.
```
**工具**：Gemini in Slides

## 部落格靈感（旅遊業範例）

```
Suggest blog post topics that would be interesting for people passionate
about travel and the tourism industry. Here's what I want you to focus on:
Make the topics unique. Keep the topics relevant. I'd like each topic to
include:
Target audience: Who would this topic specifically appeal to?
Content outline: A few bullet points with the main ideas.
Call to action: Suggest one way to engage the reader at the end.
```
